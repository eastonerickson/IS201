WHY THE TACOMA BUILD?


Off roading has always been something to catch my eye. Growing up, I always dreamed of owning my own off-raoding rig. One that can climb mounatins, plow rivers, and pull its weight. The reason I became so interested in the Toyota line up is because of the dependability and looks of the Tacoma truck. I fell in love. It was also very convincing to learn of all the National Parks near where I live where I could take my truck camping and off-roading. This Tableau graph, created by Huangyu Ju, Lian Duan, and Wenwen Yang gives a map of the National Parks in the United States. Most of these National Parks are perfect places to get my truck out in the wild and experience the off-raod world.
